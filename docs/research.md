# Electric Forest 2026 — Per-Option Camping & Lodging Packing Research (Everything Except A/C Landing)

## TL;DR
- Electric Forest 2026 (the **14th edition**, per Wikipedia) runs **Thursday June 25 – Sunday June 28, 2026** at Double JJ Resort, Rothbury MI; standard GA campers arrive Thursday (Wednesday June 24 = +$50 add-on), while **all Good Life, Back 40, RV, group, and hotel packages already include Wednesday arrival/check-in**. The roster is confirmed current with several 2026 changes (EFfortless→Pre-Set Tent, Sherwood Court→Good Life Tent Camping, new Good Life Pre-Set RV, new GA Preferred Car Camping, new Uplift Lounge add-on, Good Life Lounge now A/C).
- The single biggest packing driver is **what each tier PROVIDES**: GA tiers provide essentially nothing but shared facilities; "GA Enhanced" (Higher Love, Maplewoods, Lucky Lake, Pre-Set Tent) and all Good Life tiers add A/C restrooms + complimentary showers; Pre-Set tents/RVs and cabins/log homes provide bedding/furniture (and sometimes towels and A/C), removing the most gear. **Enchanted Experience needs the least; GA tent the most.**
- Pack for a **warm, humid, storm-prone weekend**: highs ~74–78°F, lows ~55–59°F, daily thunderstorm potential, with a real severe-storm/evacuation history (the 2024 festival evacuated twice and the 2025 edition also saw weather disruptions). Glass is banned everywhere; the venue daypack rules are strict (empty bottles, no chairs, no pro cameras). **Bunk Police will NOT have an on-site booth in 2026**, so any drug-checking supplies must be sourced before arrival.

## Shared Context (applies to ALL options below)

### Weather "pack-for" guidance (refreshed June 18, 2026 — one week out)
**Climatology (late-June Rothbury 49452):** typical highs ~74–78°F, lows ~55–60°F; June day-highs range 68–88°F and lows 52–69°F (AccuWeather); ~14 rain days/month totaling ~2.5", humidity ~80%, UV index ~5 (weather-us.com); ~15.5 hrs daylight (sunrise ~6:05 AM, sunset ~9:30 PM); no snow May–September. The severe-storm pattern is real and recurring.

**Current forecast for the festival dates** (synthesized from weather.com, FOX Weather, and NWS Grand Rapids, pulled June 17–18, 2026):
- weather.com daily: **Thu 6/25 ~74°/55°, Fri 6/26 ~78°/57°, Sat 6/27 ~78°/59°, Sun 6/28 ~77°/58°.**
- FOX Weather trends slightly cooler (highs low-70s, lows low-to-mid 50s).
- NWS Grand Rapids (GRR) shows an active, unsettled pattern this week with repeated rounds of showers and thunderstorms across West Michigan and gusty winds (gusts to 30+ mph in some periods); the office's current messaging reiterates "When Thunder Roars, Go Indoors."
- Sources disagree on exact warmth (low-70s vs near-80) — normal a week out. **Plan for warm, humid days near 80°F, cool nights in the mid-to-upper 50s, and a high likelihood of at least one thunderstorm.**

**Pack-for recommendation (everyone):** sun protection (SPF, hat, sunglasses, bandana), rain protection (poncho/rain jacket, pack-cover, extra tarp, dry bag), warm layer/hoodie for nights, sturdy closed-toe shoes plus a rinse-off pair, and a documented severe-weather plan. **In a storm, shelter in a hard vehicle or a shuttle/shelter bus — NOT a tent** (Michigan State Police Lt. Robinson, via WZZM13: "do not try to shelter in your tent. Go into your vehicle. You're a lot safer inside your vehicle"). Stake/guy-out everything; secure pop-ups, tarps, and loose items. Monitor the EF app and 99.5 FM.

### Prohibited items (2026 official "Festival Info & Policies")
**Banned everywhere (campground + venue):** fireworks/sky lanterns/explosives; grills, open flame, candles, charcoal (small Coleman-style camp stoves with **≤1 lb propane canisters** are the only exemption); generators (RV built-in gas generators and solar generators OK; external gas/diesel/kerosene generators and any gas/fuel cans banned); **glass of any kind** (mirrors OK); kegs/hard liquor; drones; laser pointers; nitrous oxide; weapons of any kind; tiki torches; golf clubs; **hammers (bring a rubber mallet instead)**; helium tanks; bicycles/skateboards/scooters/one-wheels; boats/hard floatation; Native American headdresses; Confederate flags; threatening signs/apparel.
**Additionally banned IN the venue:** outside food/alcohol/beverage; chairs/lawn/beach furniture (inflatable WindPouch-type seating that needs no pump is allowed, except on the Ranch Arena lawn); pro/audio/detachable-lens cameras; knives of any kind; framed/large backpacks; picnic baskets; tents; wagons/strollers/carts; frisbees; large markers/paint pens/spray paint; soliciting/sampling.
**Permitted venue daypack:** small non-framed bag/fanny pack/small hydration pack; **empty** reusable bottle/hydration pack (or one factory-sealed water bottle ≤1 gallon); phone + power bank; ID + card; sunscreen/lip balm/sunglasses/bandana; warm layer; earplugs; small handmade gifts/pins/kandi for trading; **totem ≤7 ft on light PVC (no wood/metal, nothing sharp on top, no threatening messaging)**; LED accessories; biodegradable glitter; **factory-sealed single-dose naloxone nasal spray (4 mg) is permitted.**
**Alcohol (campgrounds, 21+):** one case of beer/seltzer OR one box of wine per person; no glass, no liquor, no kegs; cannot be carried into the venue.
**Medications:** keep prescriptions in original labeled bottles; ID must match; declare at search.
**Pets:** service animals only; local kennels listed by HQ.
**Sound systems:** no speaker larger than 11x5x5; handheld/USB personal speakers OK.

### Logistics & policies affecting packing (2026)
- **RFID wristband:** register in the EF app (AXS login; 6-letter code on badge underside) BEFORE putting it on; each attendee registers their own. Registration is required.
- **Vehicle/parking pass:** apply to the **exterior, upper driver-side windshield** before arriving in Rothbury (not the inside); one pass per vehicle, not per person.
- **Cashless on-site:** all major debit/credit incl. international cards; **no cash at vendors** — carry a backup card.
- **No driving in/out during the event** except medical/emergency, and only **8 AM–8 PM ET** with wristband scan + re-inspection; drive slowly, pedestrians have right of way.
- **Water:** free refill stations throughout campgrounds and venue; campground water unlimited; **bottles/hydration packs must enter the venue EMPTY** (or one factory-sealed bottle ≤1 gal).
- **Ice & supplies:** sold at General Stores and Neighborhood Pods (in-camp at Lucky Lake); General Stores stock forgotten basics; a free Walmart shuttle has run in recent years (closest big-box ~23 mi in Muskegon).
- **Showers:** GA campgrounds have shared shower facilities; GA Enhanced (Higher Love, Maplewoods, Lucky Lake, Pre-Set Tent) and all Good Life tiers include **complimentary showers + A/C restrooms** (typical hours 8 AM–8 PM).
- **Shuttles:** Good Life 24-hr Express connects Good Life Village ↔ Back 40 ↔ GA Campgrounds ↔ Gold Rush Water Park/Frontier Suites; Camp Higher Love and Lucky Lake have dedicated shuttles; Enchanted has a golf-cart shuttle; regional shuttles run from Grand Rapids, Detroit, Chicago, and New York City (via bus.com).
- **Cell service degrades at peak.** An estimated 40,000–50,000 people attended in 2025 (Wikipedia); the venue is large (Double JJ Resort spans ~2,000 acres). Pre-download the app/maps, set a meetup spot, drop a pin on your campsite, consider walkie-talkies. (Note: Rothbury's amended permit allows up to 70,000 at any point, but AEG has said attendance will not increase for the foreseeable future.)
- **Harm reduction 2026:** **Bunk Police will NOT have an on-site booth this year** — source any fentanyl/xylazine test strips and reagent/purity kits BEFORE arrival; keep prescriptions labeled; factory-sealed naloxone permitted.
- **ADA:** contact access@electricforestfestival.com in advance; Access Center on-site; powered 3-/4-wheel mobility devices with safety features allowed (no personal golf carts/ATVs/two-wheel scooters/one-wheels).

### Shared supporting data blocks

## Details — Per-Option Breakdown

> For each: **PROVIDES** = inclusions that let you REMOVE items from a master camping list. **OPTION-SPECIFIC** = what is unique or especially important to bring. A standard "master camping kit" (BYO tent options) = tent + rubber mallet/stakes, sleeping bag + pad/air mattress, pillow, camp chair, shade canopy, cooler + ice plan, headlamp/lanterns, power banks, food + ≤1lb-canister stove, water jugs, toiletries + quick-dry towel + shower shoes, trash bags, sunscreen/bug spray, layers/rain gear.

### GROUP A — GA Tent & Car Camping (most to pack)
**1. GA Tent Camping** — *Thursday arrival (Wed +$50); parking sold separately.*
- PROVIDES: GA campground access; **shared restrooms + shower facilities** (basic, not A/C); Main Street general store/ice/water refill.
- OPTION-SPECIFIC / BRING: Everything — full master camping kit. **Buy a parking pass separately** (or arrive with a multi-person car). Bring quick-dry towel + shower shoes (shared showers). For the campground haul, a wagon/cart is allowed (carts are banned only IN the venue). Bring extra shade — limited tree cover.

**2. GA Car Camping** — *BYO tent at car, site ~12'×35'; Thu (Wed +$50).*
- PROVIDES: Same shared facilities as GA tent; **car beside your tent** (no separate parking pass needed; the car camping pass IS the site).
- OPTION-SPECIFIC / BRING: Full master kit; pack heavier since the car is on-site (bigger cooler, more water, car-powered inflator). Narrow site (~12 ft wide) — a low-profile canopy fits better than a large pavilion.

**3. GA Preferred Car Camping** *(new 2026)* — *guaranteed site near main entrance, Wednesday arrival included, site ~20'×25'.*
- PROVIDES: Shared facilities; **larger site (20'×25')**; **Wednesday arrival included**; guaranteed location near the main venue entrance regardless of arrival time.
- OPTION-SPECIFIC / BRING: Full master kit; the extra space supports a full canopy + larger group setup. Otherwise identical to GA car camping.

### GROUP B — GA RV
**4. GA RV** — *BYO RV, Wednesday arrival; includes 1 companion car (<16 ft) pass.*
- PROVIDES: One RV site; companion-car pass; shared GA facilities. **No electrical hookup.**
- OPTION-SPECIFIC / BRING: Everything to run the RV self-contained — **fresh water (no hookup), full propane for onboard systems, and battery/solar power** (external gas generators banned; RV built-in gas generators and solar OK). Leveling blocks, grey/black-water plan (no on-site dump). Bring a venue daypack.

**5. GA Powered RV** — *BYO RV + 30A electrical + companion car, Wednesday.*

## Recommendations

**Stage 1 — Identify your tier's "provides" and delete from the master list now.** Hotel and Enchanted holders should pack a daypack + clothes and almost nothing else. Pre-Set RV (Lucky Lake/Good Life) and Pre-Set Tent (GA/Electric Avenue/Bungalow) holders should delete shelter + sleeping gear, then re-add the **option-specific gaps** (fan + towels for Electric Avenue/Bungalow; coffee/food/water for Pre-Set RV). BYO-tent tiers (GA, Maplewoods, Higher Love, Lucky Lake, Good Life tent/car, Back 40 camping) keep the full master kit.

**Stage 2 — Layer in the universal weather/safety kit regardless of tier:** poncho/rain jacket, warm night layer, SPF/hat/sunglasses, sturdy + rinse shoes, earplugs, power bank, empty refillable bottle, electrolytes, and a written severe-weather plan (shelter in vehicle/bus, not tent). This is non-negotiable given the recurring evacuation history — the 2024 festival evacuated twice (Saturday ~7:48 PM and again Sunday ~12:30 AM, ending the event early) and 2025 also saw early-arrival weather disruptions.

**Stage 3 — Pre-arrival admin (all tiers):** register every wristband in the EF app before wearing; apply the vehicle pass to the exterior upper driver-side windshield; load a backup card (cashless); pre-download maps + set a meetup spot; **order any drug-checking supplies before arrival (Bunk Police not on-site in 2026)**; keep meds in labeled bottles; pack factory-sealed naloxone if desired.

**Decision thresholds that change the plan:**
- **If NWS issues a Severe Thunderstorm/Tornado Watch for Oceana County during the event** → pre-stage a go-bag in your hard vehicle, move valuables/electronics inside, and over-stake all structures before the front arrives.
- **If the forecast trends toward upper-80s** (some sources hint warmer) → add extra water, a second fan/battery, and electrolyte supplies; for Electric Avenue/Bungalow (no A/C) the fan becomes critical, not optional.
- **For Back 40 Cabin/Log Home** → if you cannot get written confirmation from the Good Life concierge that linens/towels are included, **pack them**; the cost of being wrong (no bedding at a sold-out festival) outweighs the bag space.

## Caveats
- **Festival is sold out;** most packages showed "Sold Out"/resale-only at research time. Inclusions still apply to ticket holders.
- **Open verification items:**
  - **(a) Back 40 Cabin & Log Home linens/bedding + towels: NOT confirmed by Electric Forest.** EF's official copy is silent (every other Good Life lodging type explicitly lists bedding/towels). Strong indirect evidence (Double JJ Resort + commercial rental listings of the same units state "linens & towels provided") suggests they ARE supplied, but treat as unconfirmed and bring backup.
  - **(b) In-tent outlet power tolerance (Electric Avenue, Bungalow): no published wattage/amperage.** EF states only "outlet in tent for charging small electronic devices." A low-draw fan + phone charging + small light is very likely fine; high-draw appliances are not intended. Not officially quantified.
  - **(c) Pre-Set RV daily service:** confirmed — waste removal + water refill once per festival day Thu–Sun; 30A power.
- **The electricforest.com/welcome ("complete guide") page still shows 2025 arrival dates** (e.g., "Thursday, June 19," "Tuesday 6/17," "Wednesday 6/18") — these are stale cached 2025 figures. The **correct 2026 dates are on the pass-types page** and used throughout this report (Thu June 25; Wed June 24 early arrival; Back 40 lodging Wed June 24 3 PM; box office opens Tue June 23 10 PM).
- **Weather is a forecast** a week out; sources disagree (low-70s vs near-80, exact storm timing). Treat daily highs/lows as estimates and re-check the EF app and NWS GRR closer to and during the event.
- **Uplift Lounge** is an in-venue A/C add-on, not lodging; it does not alter a camping packing list beyond providing an A/C refuge, restrooms, water, and charging for holders.
