## 🚫 Do NOT pack (confiscated at the gate)

**Banned everywhere:** Fireworks, sky lanterns, explosives; Grills, open flame, candles, charcoal (small Coleman-style camp stoves with ≤1 lb propane canisters are the only exemption); Generators (RV built-in gas generators and solar generators OK; external gas/diesel/kerosene generators and any gas/fuel cans banned); Glass of any kind (mirrors OK); Kegs and hard liquor; Drones; Laser pointers; Nitrous oxide; Weapons of any kind; Tiki torches; Golf clubs; Hammers (bring a rubber mallet instead); Helium tanks; Bicycles, skateboards, scooters, one-wheels; Boats and hard floatation; Native American headdresses; Confederate flags; Threatening signs or apparel; Speakers larger than 11x5x5 (handheld/USB personal speakers OK).

**Also banned in the venue:** Outside food, alcohol, or beverage; Chairs, lawn, or beach furniture (no-pump inflatable WindPouch-type seating allowed, except on the Ranch Arena lawn); Pro / audio / detachable-lens cameras; Knives of any kind; Framed or large backpacks; Picnic baskets; Tents; Wagons, strollers, carts; Frisbees; Large markers, paint pens, spray paint; Soliciting or sampling.

**Permitted venue daypack:** Small non-framed bag, fanny pack, or small hydration pack; Empty reusable bottle/hydration pack (or one factory-sealed water bottle ≤1 gallon); Phone + power bank; ID + card; Sunscreen, lip balm, sunglasses, bandana; Warm layer; Earplugs; Small handmade gifts, pins, kandi for trading; Totem ≤7 ft on light PVC (no wood/metal, nothing sharp on top, no threatening messaging); LED accessories; Biodegradable glitter; Factory-sealed single-dose naloxone nasal spray (4 mg).

**Alcohol (campground, 21+):** Campgrounds, 21+: one case of beer/seltzer OR one box of wine per person; no glass, no liquor, no kegs; cannot be carried into the venue.

**Meds:** Keep prescriptions in original labeled bottles; ID must match; declare at search.

**Harm reduction:** Bunk Police will NOT be on-site in 2026 — source any fentanyl/xylazine test strips and reagent/purity kits before arrival; keep prescriptions labeled; factory-sealed single-dose naloxone nasal spray is permitted.
